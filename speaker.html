Loudness & Clarity (Measured in dB)

Speaker loudness is measured in decibels (dB), with clarity being equally important:

  • Budget phones: ~70-80 dB
  • Mid-range phones: 80-90 dB
  • Flagship phones: 90+ dB (e.g., iPhone 15, Galaxy S-series)
