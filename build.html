Vegan Leather in Smartphones

What is Vegan Leather? Vegan leather is a sustainable alternative to traditional leather made from plant-based materials like pineapple fibers, apple peels, cactus, or mushrooms. It's a cruelty-free, eco-friendly option with a premium feel.

Benefits: Vegan leather is eco-friendly, stylish, and durable. It provides a luxurious, textured finish without the environmental impact of traditional leather. However, it may not last as long as real leather.

Examples of Vegan Leather in Smartphones:

  • Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 4: Vegan leather back on select editions.
  • Realme GT Master Edition: Vegan leather for a high-end look.
  • Motorola Edge Series: Vegan leather edition for an eco-conscious approach.

Water and Dust Resistance (IP Rating)

IP Rating (Ingress Protection): IP ratings show how well a device resists dust and water. A higher number means better protection.

IP67: Dustproof and can survive submersion in 1 meter of water for 30 minutes. Example: Google Pixel 7, Samsung Galaxy A54.

IP68: More water-resistant, with protection against submersion in more than 1 meter of water. Example: Samsung Galaxy S25, iPhone 16 Pro Max.

IP69: Complete dustproof and high-pressure water-resistant. Found in rugged phones for industrial use. Example: Cat S62 Pro, Oppo F27+.
